Distance & Slope

Homework

Distance: $Δy$ always positive (absolute value)
Distance Formula: $d=√{(x_2-x_1 )^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}$
Distance Formula = Pythagorean Theorem
$c^2=a^2+b^2$
$d^2=(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2$
Slope Formula:${Δy}/{Δx}={y_2-y_1}/{x_2-x_1}$, where $x_2≠x_1$
Midpoint Formula:$({x_1+x_2}/2$, ${y_1+y_2}/2)$
Collinear Points lie on the same line slope between any two points is equal
Ex) Given 3 collinear points below, find the value of $k$
$a=(7,-2)$
$b=(0,5)$
$c=(3,k)$

$m_{ab}={y_2-y_1}/{x_2-x_1}$
$={5--2}/{0-7}$
$={5+2}/{0-7}$
$=-7/7=-1$

$m_{ab}=m_{ac}$
$-1={k-5}/{3-0}$
$-1={k-5}/3$
$-3=k-5$

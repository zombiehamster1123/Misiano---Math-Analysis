Parallelogram

To prove vertices form a parallelogram ‘H you must do one of the following:
1) Show diagonals bisect each other (use Midpoint)
2) Show opposite sides are parallel and congruent (use Slope & Distance)
3) Show both pairs of opposite sides are congruent (use Distance)
4) Show both pairs of opposite sides are parallel (use Slope)

Rhombus

To prove vertices form a rhombus ‘H
1) Show diagonals of a parallelogram (prove parallelogram) are perpendicular to each other (use Slope)
OR
2) show all sides are congruent (use Distance)

Rectangle

To prove vertices form a rectangle
1) Show diagonals of a parallelogram (prove parallelogram) are congruent (use Distance)
OR
2) Show a parallelogram with one right angle (use Slope)

Square

To prove vertices form a square ‘H
1) Show all sides are congruent (equilateral) (use Distance) with one right angle (use Slope)
OR
2) Show a rectangle (prove rectangle) with consecutive sides congruent (use Distance)

Right Triangle

To prove vertices form a right triangle ‘H
1) Show one right angle (use slope)

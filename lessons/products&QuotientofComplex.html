Products & Quotients of Complex Numbers in Polar Form

Products of Complex #s in Polar Form

$\table r_1(\cosθ_1+\i\sinθ)•r_2(\cosθ+\i\sinθ_2); =r_{1}r_{2}[\cos(θ_1+θ_2)+\i\sin(θ_1+θ_2)]$

Example

$\table 3(\cos120°+\i\sin120°)•5(\cos45°+\i\sin45°); =(3)(5)[\cos(120°+45°)+\i\sin(120°+45°)]; =15(\cos165°+\i\sin165°); =15\cis165°$

Quotients of Complex #s in Polar Form

${r_1(\cosθ_1+\i\sinθ_1)}/{r_2(\cosθ_2+\i\sinθ_2)}={r_1}/{r_2}[\cos(θ_1-θ_2)+\i\sin(θ_1-θ_2)]$

Example

$\table {3(\cos120°+\i\sin120°)}/{5(\cos45°+\i\sin45°)}=3/5[\cos(120°-45°)+\i\sin(120°-45°)]; =3/5(\cos75°+\i\sin75°); =3/5\cis75°$

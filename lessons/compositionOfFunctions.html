Inverse Functions
$f(x)$ & $g(x)$ are inverse functions when
$(f∘g)(x)=x$
$(f∘g)(x)=x$

If it exists, the inverse funciton of:
$f(x)=f^{-1}(x)$ & $g(x)=g^{-1}(x)$

If f(x) & g(x) are inverse functions, then
$f(x)=g^{-1}(x)$ & $g(x)=f^{-1}(x)$
Ex) Are these functions inverse of each other?
$f(x)={x-1}/{2}$
$g(x)=2x+1$

$(f∘g)(x)=f(g(x))={(2x+1)-1}/{2}={2x}/{2}=x$
$(g∘f)(x)=g(f(x))=2({x-1}/2)+1=x-1+1=x$
Since$(f∘g)(x)=(g∘f)(x)=x$, $f(x)$ & $g(x)$ are inverse functions

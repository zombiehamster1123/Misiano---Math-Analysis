Arithmetic Sequences & Series

Sequence - a set of #s in a certain order
Terms - the #s in the sequence
$\table a_1 \text",", a_2 \text",", ... \text",", a_n; 1^{st}\text"term" , 2^{nd}\text"term",, n^{th}\text"term"$

Arithimetic Sequence - each term after $a_1$ equals the sum of the preceding term + a common difference, $d$
$\table a_1, \text", ", a_2, \text", ", a_3, \text", ", ...; a_1, , a_{1}+d, , a_{1}+2d$

Common Difference, d - the difference between successive terms of an arithmetic sequence

ex) Find the next 3 terms in the arithmetic sequence...
$\table x-4\text", " x+3\text", " x+10\text", ..."; d=7; (x+10)+7=x+17; (x+17)+7=x+24; (x+24)+7=x+31$

The $n^{th}$ term of an Arithmetic Sequence
$a_{n}=a_{1}+(n-1)d$

ex) Find the $58^{th}$ term in the sequence, 10, 4, -2, ...
$\table d=-6; a_{n}=a_{1}+(n-1)d; a_{58}=10+(58-1)(-6); a_{58}=-332$

Arithmetic Means - the terms between any two nonconsecutive terms of an arithmetic sequence

ex) Form an arithmetic sequence that has 2 arithmeitc means between -4 & 5
Find $d$... $\table a_{n}=a_{1}+(n-1)d; 5=-4+(4-1)d; 9=3d; 3=d$ $\table a_2\text":", -4+3=-1; a_3\text":", -1+3=2$
This sequence will have the form: $\table -4\text", ", -1\text", ", 2\text", ", 5; a_1\text", ", a_2\text", ", a_3\text", ", a_4$

Arithmetic Series - the sum of the terms of an arithmetic sequence
$\table \text"arithmetic sequence", →, \text"arithmetic series"; 1\text", " 3\text", " 5\text", " 7\text", " 9, =, 1+3+5+7+9; a_1\text", " a_2\text", "\text" ... " a_n, =, a_1+a_2+...+a_n$

The sum of the $1^{st}$ 'n' terms of an arithmetic series
$\table \text"... when the last term is known", S_n=n/2(a_1+a_n); \html'... when the last term is <b>not</b> known', S_n=n/2[2a_{1}+(n-1)d]$
ex) Find the sum of the first 11 terms in the series: -3-1+1+3+...+$a_{11}$
$\table S_n=n/2[2a_{1}+(n-1)d]; S_{11}={11}/2[2(-3)+(11-1)(2)]; S_{11}=77$

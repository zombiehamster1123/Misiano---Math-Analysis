The Parabola


Vertex: midpoint between focus & directrix

Directrix parallel to y-axis: $(y-k)^2=4p(x-h)$
Directrix parallel to x-axis: $(x-h)^2=4p(y-k)$
$vertex=(h,k)$
p = length from vertex to focus
for $x^2$ for $y^2$
p is +: parabola opens up p is -: parabola opens down
p is +: parabola opens right p is -: parabola opens lieft

General Form of the Equation of a Parabola
directrix parallel to y-axis: $y^2+Dx+Ey+F=0$
directrix parallel to x-axis: $x^2+Dx+Ey+F=0$

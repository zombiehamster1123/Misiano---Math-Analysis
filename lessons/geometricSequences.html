Geometric Sequences & Series

Sequence - a set of #s in a certain order
Terms - the #s in the sequence
$\table a_1, \text", ", a_2, \text"," ..., \text", ", a_n; 1^{st}\text"term", , 2^{nd}\text"term", , , n^{th}\text"term"$

Geometric Sequence - each term after $a_1$ equals the product of the preceding term & a common ratio, $r$
$\table a_1, \text", ", a_2, \text", ", a_3, \text", ", ...; a_1, , a_{1}r, , a_{1}r^2$

Common Ration, r - the ratio of successive terms in a geometric sequence
$r={a_n}/{a_{n-1}}$
ex) Find the next 2 terms in the geometric sequence...
$4x, 12x, 36x, 108x, 324x, ...$
$\table \text"Find r..."; r={a_n}/{a_{n-1}}; r={12x}/{4x}=3\text" or "r={36x}/{12x}=3$ $\table a_4=a_{3}r=36x(3)=108x; a_5=a_{5}r=108x(3)=324x; OR; a_4=a_{1}r^3=4x(3^3)=108x; a_5=a_{1}r^4=4x(3^4)=324x$

Recall... $\table a_1, \text", ", a_2, \text", ", a_3, \text", ", ..., \text", ", a_n; 1^{st}\text"term", , 2^{nd}\text"term", , 3^{rd}\text"term", , , , n^{th}\text"term"$

The $n^{th}$ term of a Geometric Sequence
$a_n=a_{1}r^{(n-1)}$
ex) Find the $9^{th}$ term of the geometric sequence: $16,-8,4,-2,1,...$
$\table \text"Find r..."; r={-8}/{16}=-1/2; OR; r=4/{-8}=-1/2$ $\table a_n=a_1r^{(n-1)}; a_9=16(-1/2)^{(9-1)}; a_9=1/16=0.0625$

Geometric Means - the terms between any two nonconsecutive terms of a geometric sequence
ex) Form a sequence that has 2 geometric means between 1 & 27
$\table \text"Find r..."; a_n=a_{1}r^{(n-1)}; 27=1r^{(4-1)}; ∛\ov{27}=r; 3=r$ $\table a_2:, 1(2)^1=3; a_3:, 1(3)^2=9$ $\table 1\text",", 3\text",", 9\text",", 27; a_1\text",", a_2\text",", a_3\text",", a_4$

Geometric Series - the sum of the terms of a geometric sequence
$\table \text"geometric sequence", →, \text"geometric series"; 1\text", " 5\text", " 25\text", " 125, =, 1+5+25+125; a_1\text", " a_2\text", "\text" ..., " a_n, =, a_1+a_2+...+a_n$

The sum of the $1^{st}$ 'n; terms of a geometric series: $S_n={a_1-a_{1}r^n}/{1-r}. r≠1$
ex) Find the sum of the first 7 terms in the series... $1/2+1/4+1/8+...$
$\table ,\text"Find r...", ; ,r={a_n}/{a_{n-1}}, ; r{1/4}/{1/2}=1/2, \text"OR", r={1/8}/{1/4}=1/2$     $\table S_7={1/2-1/2(1/2)^7}/{1-1/2}; S_7={1/2-1/{256}}/{1-1/2}={128-1}/{256-128}={127}/{128}$

Infinite Sequences & Series

Infinite Sequence - a set of infinitely many terms
for $a_n>a_{n+1}$... an n approaches ∞, $a_n$ approaches 0 $\table a_1, \text", ", a_2, \text", ", ..., \text", " a_n; 1^{st}\text"term", , 2^{nd}\text"term", , , n^{th}\text"term, where n approaches ∞"$

Infinite Sequence - as $n→∞, a_n→0$
$\lim↙{n→∞}{1/n}=0$ read... "the limit of 1 over n, as n approaches infinity, equals zero"

Sum of an Infinite Series

Sum of an Infinite Geometric Series
$\table S_n={a_1}/{1-r}, |r|<1$
think about it... when $|r|>1$, the sum approaches ±∞ (a.k.a. the limit d.n.e.)
ex) Find the sum of the series: $1/{25}+1/{250}+1/{2500}+...$
$\table , \text"Find r...", ; r={1/250}/{1/25}=1/10, OR, r={1/2500}/{1/250}=1/10; , \text"Since |"r\text"|",1\text", "S_n={a_1}/{1-r}$

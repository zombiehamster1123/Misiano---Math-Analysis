Exponential & Logarithmic Equations & Inequalities

Using Logs to Solve Exp Equations
Solve for x to the nearest thousandth...
$\table 3.9^{x+1}=28; \log3.9^{x+1}=\log28; (x+1)\log3.9=\log28; x={\log28}/{\log3.9}-1; x≈1.448$

Solve for x to the nearest thousandth...
$\table 2^{x+5}>3^{x-2}; x(\log2-\log3)>\log3^{-2}-\log2^5; x(\log2/3)>\log{3^{-2}}/{2^5}; x<{\log{3^{-2}}/{2^5}}/{\log2/3}; x<13.967$
